How access works
Access to SubSocial is managed directly in Supabase Authentication. Each team member signs in with their own email and password — there are no in-app invite flows or role assignments here.

To invite a new team member, go to the Supabase dashboard and use the built-in invite function. They'll receive a login link by email and can set their own password on first sign-in.
Open Supabase Auth dashboard →
Inviting a team member
1
Open the Supabase Auth → Users page.
2
Click Invite user and enter their email address.
3
They'll receive a magic link by email. On first sign-in they can set their password.
4
They'll land on SubSocial at subsocial-iota.vercel.app and have full access.
